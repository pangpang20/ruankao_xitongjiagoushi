## Introduction | 概述

### What are Information Security Laws? | 什么是信息系统安全法规?

**English:**

**Information Security Laws** are legal frameworks that govern the protection, processing, storage, and transmission of information in information systems. They establish obligations for organizations, rights for individuals, and enforcement mechanisms for regulators.

**Historical Evolution | 历史演进**:

```
1980s: Early computer crime laws
       早期计算机犯罪法
       - US Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (1986)
       - 美国计算机欺诈和滥用法（1986）

1990s: Internet era privacy laws
       互联网时代隐私法
       - EU Data Protection Directive (1995)
       - 欧盟数据保护指令（1995）

2000s: Sector-specific regulations
       行业特定法规
       - HIPAA (Healthcare), SOX (Finance), PCI DSS (Payments)
       - HIPAA（医疗）、SOX（金融）、PCI DSS（支付）

2010s: Comprehensive cybersecurity laws
       综合性网络安全法
       - China Cybersecurity Law (2017)
       - 中国网络安全法（2017）

2018: Global privacy breakthrough
      全球隐私突破
       - GDPR (EU) - Extraterritorial effect
       - GDPR（欧盟）- 域外效力
       - California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
       - 加州消费者隐私法（CCPA）

2021-2023: China's data governance trilogy
           中国数据治理三部曲
       - Data Security Law (2021)
       - 数据安全法（2021）
       - Personal Information Protection Law (2021)
       - 个人信息保护法（2021）
       - Cross-Border Data Transfer Regulations (2022)
       - 数据出境安全评估办法（2022）
```

**Why Information Security Laws Matter | 信息安全法规的重要性**:

**For National Security | 对国家安全**:
- **Critical Infrastructure Protection**: Power grids, financial systems, telecommunications
- **关键基础设施保护**: 电网、金融系统、通信
- **Data Sovereignty**: Control over national data resources
- **数据主权**: 对国家数据资源的控制
- **Cybersecurity Defense**: Legal basis for cyber incident response
- **网络安全防御**: 网络事件响应的法律基础

**For Individuals | 对个人**:
- **Privacy Rights**: Control over personal data
- **隐私权**: 对个人数据的控制
- **Legal Remedies**: Right to seek damages for privacy violations
- **法律救济**: 隐私侵犯的损害赔偿权
- **Data Portability**: Right to move data between services
- **数据可携带性**: 在服务间移动数据的权利

**For Organizations | 对组织**:
- **Compliance Obligation**: Legal requirements to protect data
- **合规义务**: 保护数据的法律要求
- **Reputation Management**: Customer trust through compliance
- **声誉管理**: 通过合规建立客户信任
- **Risk Mitigation**: Avoid legal penalties (GDPR: up to €20M or 4% of global revenue)
- **风险缓解**: 避免法律处罚（GDPR: 最高2000万欧元或全球营业额4%）

**For System Architects Exam | 对系统架构设计师考试**:
- **Core Topic**: Laws account for 8-12% of exam questions
- **核心主题**: 法律占考试问题的8-12%
- **Practical Application**: Architecture decisions must comply with laws
- **实践应用**: 架构决策必须符合法律
- **Interdisciplinary**: Combines law, technology, and management
- **跨学科**: 结合法律、技术和管理

**中文:**

**信息系统安全法规**是管理信息系统中信息的保护、处理、存储和传输的法律框架。它们为组织建立义务，为个人赋予权利，并为监管机构提供执法机制。

## China's Cybersecurity Legal System | 中国网络安全法律法规体系

### 2.1 Cybersecurity Law | 网络安全法

**English:**

The **Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China** (enacted June 1, 2017) is China's foundational law for cybersecurity.

**Legislative Background | 立法背景**:
- Respond to increasing cyber threats
- 应对日益增长的网络威胁
- Protect critical information infrastructure
- 保护关键信息基础设施
- Safeguard national security and public interests
- 维护国家安全和公共利益

**中文:**

《中华人民共和国网络安全法》（2017年6月1日施行）是中国网络安全的基础性法律。

#### 2.1.1 Core Provisions | 核心条款

**Article 21: Classified Protection System | 第二十一条：等级保护制度** **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**English:**

The State implements a classified protection system for cybersecurity. Network operators shall, in accordance with the requirements of the classified protection system:

1. **Formulate internal security management systems and operating procedures**
   制定内部安全管理制度和操作规程
2. **Determine the person responsible for cybersecurity**
   确定网络安全负责人
3. **Adopt technical measures to prevent computer viruses, network attacks, and network intrusions**
   采取防范计算机病毒和网络攻击、网络侵入等危害网络安全行为的技术措施
4. **Adopt technical measures to monitor and record the operational status of networks and cybersecurity incidents**
   采取监测、记录网络运行状态、网络安全事件的技术措施，并按照规定留存相关的网络日志不少于六个月
5. **Adopt data classification, important data backup, and encryption measures**
   采取数据分类、重要数据备份和加密等措施

**Article 37: Critical Information Infrastructure Protection | 第三十七条：关键信息基础设施保护** **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**English:**

Critical information infrastructure (CII) operators that purchase network products and services that may affect national security shall undergo national security review.

**Critical Information Infrastructure Sectors | 关键信息基础设施行业**:

```
Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) Sectors:
关键信息基础设施行业:

1. Public Communication and Information Services
   公共通信和信息服务
   • Telecommunications (电信)
   • Internet (互联网)
   • Broadcasting (广播电视)

2. Energy
   能源
   • Electricity (电力)
   • Oil and gas (石油天然气)

3. Transportation
   交通运输
   • Railways (铁路)
   • Civil aviation (民航)
   • Highways (公路)

4. Finance
   金融
   • Banking (银行)
   • Securities (证券)
   • Insurance (保险)

5. Public Services
   公共服务
   • Water supply (供水)
   • Gas supply (供气)
   • Healthcare (医疗卫生)

6. E-Government
   电子政务
   • Government portals (政府门户)
   • Government data centers (政府数据中心)

7. National Defense and Science & Technology
   国防科技工业
```

**Article 40-44: Real-Name Verification | 第40-44条：实名制** **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**English:**

Network operators providing:
- Internet access services
- Domain name registration services
- Fixed/mobile telephone access services
- Information publishing/instant messaging services

Must require users to provide real identity information.

**中文:**

网络运营者提供互联网接入、域名注册、固定电话和移动电话接入、信息发布或即时通讯服务时，应当要求用户提供真实身份信息。

#### 2.1.2 Legal Liability | 法律责任

**Administrative Penalties | 行政处罚** **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**:

| Violation<br/>违法行为                                                    | Penalty<br/>处罚                                                                                                                         |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Failure to fulfill security protection obligations<br/>未履行安全保护义务 | • Warning (警告)<br/>• Fine: 10,000-100,000 RMB (罚款1-10万元)<br/>• Direct responsible person: 5,000-50,000 RMB (直接负责人5千-5万元)   |
| Failure to implement real-name verification<br/>未实施实名制              | • Warning (警告)<br/>• Order to rectify (责令改正)<br/>• Refuse to rectify: Fine 50,000-500,000 RMB (拒不改正5-50万元)                   |

| Personal information violation<br/>侵犯个人信息                           | • Confiscate illegal gains (没收违法所得)<br/>• Fine: 1x-10x illegal gains or max 1,000,000 RMB (罚款1-10倍违法所得或最高100万元)        |

**Criminal Liability | 刑事责任**:

**Article 286 of Criminal Law | 刑法第286条**:
- **Crime of Destroying Computer Information Systems | 破坏计算机信息系统罪**
- Penalty: Up to 5 years imprisonment (最高5年有期徒刑)

**Article 253 of Criminal Law | 刑法第253条**:
- **Crime of Infringing on Citizens' Personal Information | 侵犯公民个人信息罪**
- Penalty: Up to 7 years imprisonment (最高7年有期徒刑)

#### 2.2.2 Data Cross-Border Transfer | 数据跨境传输 **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**Article 31: Cross-Border Data Transfer Requirements | 第31条：数据跨境传输要求**

**English:**

Critical information infrastructure operators and data processors handling important data or large volumes of personal information shall:

1. **Store data collected and generated in China within China**
   在中华人民共和国境内收集和产生的数据应当在境内存储
2. **Undergo security assessment for cross-border data transfer**
   因业务需要确需向境外提供的，应当进行安全评估

#### 2.3.2 Principles of Personal Information Processing | 个人信息处理原则 **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**English:**

**Seven Core Principles | 七大核心原则**:

1. **Legality, Legitimacy, and Necessity | 合法、正当、必要**
   - Process only when necessary for specific, clear, and reasonable purposes
   - 只有在为特定、明确、合理的目的时才能处理

2. **Openness and Transparency | 公开、透明**
   - Disclose processing rules, purposes, methods, and scope
   - 公开处理规则、目的、方式和范围

3. **Purpose Limitation | 目的明确**
   - Collection should be directly related to processing purposes
   - 收集应当与处理目的直接相关

4. **Data Minimization | 最小必要**
   - Limit to minimum scope necessary to achieve processing purposes
   - 限于实现处理目的的最小范围

5. **Accuracy and Quality | 准确、完整**
   - Ensure accuracy, avoid impacting individual rights
   - 确保准确，避免影响个人权益

6. **Security | 安全**
   - Take necessary measures to ensure security
   - 采取必要措施确保安全

7. **Accountability | 问责**
   - Processor responsible for processing activities
   - 处理者对处理活动负责

**中文:**

处理个人信息应当遵循合法、正当、必要和诚信原则。

#### 2.3.3 Legal Basis for Processing | 处理的法律依据 **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

**English:**

Personal information may be processed only under one of the following circumstances:

**中文:**

只有符合下列情形之一的，方可处理个人信息：

1. **Consent | 取得个人的同意**
   - Most common basis
   - 最常见依据
   - Must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
   - 必须是自愿、明确、知情、无歧义的

2. **Contract Performance | 为订立、履行个人作为一方当事人的合同所必需**
   - Example: E-commerce order fulfillment
   - 示例: 电商订单履行

3. **Legal Obligation | 为履行法定职责或者法定义务所必需**
   - Example: Tax authorities processing taxpayer information
   - 示例: 税务机关处理纳税人信息

4. **Public Health Emergency | 为应对突发公共卫生事件所必需**
   - Example: COVID-19 contact tracing
   - 示例: 新冠疫情接触者追踪

5. **Public Interest | 为公共利益实施新闻报道、舆论监督等行为所必需**
   - Example: Investigative journalism
   - 示例: 调查性新闻报道

6. **Legitimate Interests | 依法在合理范围内处理个人自行公开或者其他已经合法公开的个人信息**
   - Example: Processing publicly available information
   - 示例: 处理已公开的信息

## Exam Focus Points | 考试要点

### 9.1 High-Frequency Exam Points Summary | 高频考点总结

**1. Network Security Law Core Provisions | 网络安全法核心条款** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

- **Article 21**: Classified Protection System (5 obligations)
- **第21条**: 等级保护制度（5项义务）
- **Article 37**: CII Security Review
- **第37条**: 关键信息基础设施安全审查
- **Article 40-44**: Real-name Verification
- **第40-44条**: 实名制

**2. Five Levels, Five Phases of Classified Protection | 等保五级五阶段** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

**Five Levels | 五个等级**:
- Level 1: User Self-Protection (用户自主保护级)
- Level 2: System Audit Protection (系统审计保护级)
- Level 3: Security Label Protection (安全标记保护级) **[考试最常见]**
- Level 4: Structured Protection (结构化保护级)
- Level 5: Access Verification Protection (访问验证保护级)

**Five Phases | 五个阶段**: **定备建测监**
1. Grading (定级)
2. Filing (备案)
3. Construction and Rectification (建设整改)
4. Level Assessment (等级测评)
5. Supervision and Inspection (监督检查)

**3. Personal Information Protection Law | 个人信息保护法** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

**Seven Principles | 七大原则**:
1. Legality, Legitimacy, Necessity (合法、正当、必要)
2. Openness and Transparency (公开、透明)
3. Purpose Limitation (目的明确)
4. Data Minimization (最小必要)
5. Accuracy (准确、完整)
6. Security (安全)
7. Accountability (问责)

**Eight Individual Rights | 八大个人权利**:
1. Right to Know and Decision-Making (知情决定权)
2. Right to Limit or Refuse (限制或拒绝权)
3. Right of Access (查阅权)
4. Right to Correction (更正权)
5. Right to Deletion (删除权)
6. Right to Explanation (解释说明权)
7. Right to Data Portability (可携带权)
8. Right to Designate Posthumous Manager (指定遗产管理权)

**4. GDPR Seven Principles | GDPR七大原则** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

1. Lawfulness, Fairness, Transparency (合法性、公平性、透明度)
2. Purpose Limitation (目的限制)
3. Data Minimization (数据最小化)
4. Accuracy (准确性)
5. Storage Limitation (存储限制)
6. Integrity and Confidentiality (完整性和保密性)
7. Accountability (问责制)

**GDPR Penalties | GDPR处罚**:
- Tier 1: €10M or 2% of revenue
- Tier 2: €20M or 4% of revenue (更严重违规)

**5. Three-Category Cryptography | 密码三分类** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

1. **Core State Cryptography** (核心密码) - Top Secret
2. **Ordinary State Cryptography** (普通密码) - Secret and below
3. **Commercial Cryptography** (商用密码) - Non-state secrets

**SM Algorithms | SM算法**:
- SM2: Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC) - 椭圆曲线密码
- SM3: Hash Algorithm - 哈希算法
- SM4: Block Cipher - 分组密码
- SM9: Identity-Based Cryptography - 标识密码

**6. Electronic Signature Four Requirements | 电子签名四要件** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

**Memory Aid | 记忆口诀**: **专控改改**
1. **Exclusive** signature creation data (专属)
2. **Under signer's control** (控制)
3. **Alteration to signature detectable** (签名改动可发现)
4. **Alteration to data detectable** (内容改动可发现)

**7. Trade Secret Three Elements | 商业秘密三要素** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

1. **Secrecy** (秘密性) - Not known to public
2. **Commercial Value** (价值性) - Economic benefits
3. **Confidentiality Measures** (保密性) - Protection measures taken

**8. Cross-Border Data Transfer Three Mechanisms | 数据跨境传输三机制** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

1. **Security Assessment** (安全评估) - For CII, 1M+ personal info
2. **Standard Contractual Clauses** (标准合同)
3. **Personal Information Protection Certification** (个人信息保护认证)

**9. ISO 27001 PDCA Cycle | ISO 27001 PDCA循环** **[Must Memorize | 必背]**

- **Plan** (计划): Establish ISMS, risk assessment, select controls
- **Do** (实施): Implement controls, operate ISMS
- **Check** (检查): Monitor, internal audit, management review
- **Act** (改进): Corrective/preventive actions, continuous improvement

### 9.2 Typical Exam Question Types | 典型题型分析

**Question Type 1: Law Article Identification | 题型1：法律条款辨识**

**Example Question | 示例问题**:
```
Question: According to the Cybersecurity Law of China, which of the 
following is NOT an obligation of network operators under the classified 
protection system?
问题：根据《网络安全法》，以下哪项不属于网络运营者在等级保护制度下的义务？

A. Formulate internal security management systems
   制定内部安全管理制度
B. Determine the person responsible for cybersecurity
   确定网络安全负责人
C. Report all security incidents to public security organs immediately
   立即向公安机关报告所有安全事件
D. Adopt data classification and backup measures
   采取数据分类和备份措施

Answer: C
答案：C

Explanation: Article 21 lists five obligations, but does not require 
immediate reporting of ALL security incidents. Only major incidents 
require reporting.
解释：第21条列了五项义务，但并未要求立即报告所有安全事件。只有重大事件需要报告。
```

**Question Type 2: Classified Protection Level Determination | 题型2：等级保护定级判定**

**Example Question | 示例问题**:
```
Question: A large e-commerce platform processes 5 million user accounts. 
If compromised, it would cause serious harm to public interests. What 
security protection level should this system be classified as?
问题：一个大型电商平台处理500万用户账户。如果被破坏，将对公共利益造成严重损害。
该系统应定为哪个安全保护等级？

A. Level 1 (第一级)
B. Level 2 (第二级)
C. Level 3 (第三级)
D. Level 4 (第四级)

Answer: C
答案：C

Explanation: Level 3 applies to systems that, if damaged, would cause 
serious harm to public interests. This matches the description.
解释：第三级适用于被破坏后对公共利益造成严重损害的系统。这符合描述。
```

**Question Type 3: GDPR vs PIPL vs CCPA Comparison | 题型3：GDPR vs PIPL vs CCPA对比**

**Example Question | 示例问题**:
```
Question: Which of the following statements correctly compares GDPR and PIPL?
问题：以下哪项正确比较了GDPR和PIPL？

A. GDPR has 7 principles; PIPL has 7 principles
   GDPR有7个原则；PIPL有7个原则
B. GDPR requires opt-in consent; CCPA requires opt-out
   GDPR要求选择加入同意；CCPA要求选择退出
C. PIPL penalty is up to RMB 50M or 5% of revenue; GDPR is €20M or 4%
   PIPL处罚最高5000万元或5%营业额；GDPR是2000万欧元或4%
D. All of the above
   以上都是

Answer: D
答案：D

Explanation: All statements are correct. This is a common comparison question.
解释：所有表述都正确。这是常见的对比题。
```

**Question Type 4: Electronic Signature Requirements | 题型4：电子签名要件**

**Example Question | 示例问题**:
```
Question: Which of the following is NOT a requirement for a reliable 
electronic signature under China's Electronic Signature Law?
问题：根据中国《电子签名法》，以下哪项不是可靠电子签名的要求？

A. Signature creation data is exclusive to the signer
   签名制作数据专属于签名人
B. Signature creation data is under signer's control at signing time
   签署时签名制作数据由签名人控制
C. The signature must be certified by a CA
   签名必须经CA认证
D. Any alteration to the signature after signing can be detected
   签署后对签名的任何改动可被发现

Answer: C
答案：C

Explanation: Article 13 lists four requirements, but CA certification 
is not mandatory for all electronic signatures.
解释：第13条列了四个要件，但CA认证并非所有电子签名的必须要求。
```

**Question Type 5: Case Analysis | 题型5：案例分析**

**Example Question | 示例问题**:
```
Scenario: A Chinese company wants to transfer personal data of 200,000 
users to its US subsidiary for customer service purposes.
场景：一家中国公司希望将20万用户的个人数据传输给其美国子公司以提供客户服务。

Question: What compliance mechanisms are available under PIPL?
问题：根据PIPL，有哪些合规机制可用？

A. Security assessment only (仅安全评估)
B. Standard contractual clauses or certification 
   (标准合同或认证)
C. Either security assessment OR standard contract OR certification
   (安全评估、标准合同或认证任选其一)
D. No compliance mechanism required for subsidiaries
   子公司不需要合规机制

Answer: C (Note: If the company is a CII operator or processes 1M+ 
personal info, security assessment is mandatory)
答案：C（注意：如果公司是CII运营者或处理100万以上个人信息，则安全评估是强制性的）

Explanation: PIPL provides three mechanisms for cross-border transfer. 
However, threshold triggers (CII operator, 1M+ personal info) may require 
security assessment.
解释：PIPL为跨境传输提供了三种机制。然而，达到门槛（CII运营者、100万以上个人信息）可能需要安全评估。
```
