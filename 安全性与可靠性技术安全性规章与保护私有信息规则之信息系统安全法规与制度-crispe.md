### 示例1：网络安全法核心条款

```markdown
## China's Cybersecurity Law | 中国网络安全法

### Article 21: Classified Protection System | 第二十一条：等级保护制度

**English:**

The State implements a classified protection system for cybersecurity. Network operators shall, in accordance with the requirements of the classified protection system for cybersecurity:

1. Formulate internal security management systems and operating procedures
2. Determine the person responsible for cybersecurity
3. Adopt technical measures to prevent computer viruses, network attacks, and network intrusions
4. Adopt technical measures to monitor and record the operational status of the network and cybersecurity incidents
5. Adopt data classification, important data backup, and encryption measures

**中文:**

国家实行网络安全等级保护制度。网络运营者应当按照网络安全等级保护制度的要求：

1. 制定内部安全管理制度和操作规程
2. 确定网络安全负责人
3. 采取防范计算机病毒和网络攻击、网络侵入等危害网络安全行为的技术措施
4. 采取监测、记录网络运行状态、网络安全事件的技术措施
5. 采取数据分类、重要数据备份和加密等措施
```

### 示例3：GDPR核心原则

```markdown
## GDPR Core Principles | GDPR核心原则

### **[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**

1. **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency | 合法性、公平性和透明度**
   - Legal basis required for processing
   - 处理需要法律依据
   - Clear communication to data subjects
   - 向数据主体清晰告知

2. **Purpose Limitation | 目的限制**
   - Data collected for specified, explicit, legitimate purposes
   - 数据收集用于特定、明确、合法目的
   - Cannot be further processed incompatibly with original purposes
   - 不能以与原目的不相容的方式进一步处理

3. **Data Minimization | 数据最小化**
   - Adequate, relevant, limited to what is necessary
   - 充分、相关、限于必要范围

4. **Accuracy | 准确性**
   - Data must be accurate and kept up to date
   - 数据必须准确并保持更新

5. **Storage Limitation | 存储限制**
   - Kept only as long as necessary
   - 仅保留必要期限

6. **Integrity and Confidentiality | 完整性和保密性**
   - Appropriate security measures
   - 适当的安全措施
   - Protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing
   - 防止未经授权或非法处理

7. **Accountability | 问责制**
   - Controller responsible for compliance
   - 控制者负责合规
   - Must demonstrate compliance
   - 必须证明合规
```
