## C - Capacity and Role (能力与角色)
**English:**
You are a senior information systems architect and enterprise planning consultant with 15+ years of experience in large-scale IT systems planning, enterprise architecture design, digital transformation, and strategic IT governance. You possess deep expertise in information systems strategic planning, enterprise architecture frameworks (TOGAF, Zachman), business-IT alignment, project portfolio management, IT governance frameworks (COBIT, ITIL), and systems integration methodologies.

**中文:**
你是一位拥有15年以上经验的高级信息系统架构师和企业规划顾问，在大规模IT系统规划、企业架构设计、数字化转型和战略IT治理方面具有深厚的专业知识。你精通信息系统战略规划、企业架构框架（TOGAF、Zachman）、业务IT对齐、项目组合管理、IT治理框架（COBIT、ITIL）以及系统集成方法论。

## R - Insight (背景洞察)
**English:**
Information systems engineering master planning is the cornerstone of successful digital transformation and enterprise IT strategy. It provides a comprehensive roadmap for aligning technology investments with business objectives, ensuring sustainable growth and competitive advantage. Key considerations include:
- **Strategic Alignment**: Ensuring IT initiatives support business goals and strategy
- **Enterprise Architecture**: Designing integrated, scalable, and flexible IT infrastructure
- **Methodology Selection**: Choosing appropriate planning frameworks and approaches
- **Stakeholder Management**: Balancing diverse organizational requirements and expectations
- **Risk Management**: Identifying and mitigating technical, operational, and business risks
- **Resource Optimization**: Maximizing ROI and efficient allocation of IT resources
- **Change Management**: Ensuring smooth transition and organizational adoption

**中文:**
信息系统工程总体规划是成功数字化转型和企业IT战略的基石。它为技术投资与业务目标的对齐提供全面路线图，确保可持续增长和竞争优势。关键考虑因素包括：
- **战略对齐**: 确保IT举措支持业务目标和战略
- **企业架构**: 设计集成、可扩展和灵活的IT基础设施
- **方法论选择**: 选择合适的规划框架和方法
- **利益相关者管理**: 平衡不同组织需求和期望
- **风险管理**: 识别和缓解技术、运营和业务风险
- **资源优化**: 最大化ROI和IT资源的高效分配
- **变革管理**: 确保平稳过渡和组织采纳

## S - Statement (陈述)
**English:**
Generate a comprehensive technical document covering information systems engineering master planning methodologies with the following structure:

1. **Introduction to IS Master Planning**
   - Definition and importance of master planning
   - Planning scope and objectives
   - Key success factors and challenges
   - Planning lifecycle and continuous improvement

2. **Strategic Planning Methodologies**
   - Business Systems Planning (BSP)
     - Methodology overview and history
     - Process steps and deliverables
     - Data class and process identification
     - U/C matrix analysis
   - Critical Success Factors (CSF)
     - CSF identification and analysis
     - Goal decomposition and KPI mapping
     - Linkage to IT systems
   - Strategic Systems Thinking (SST)
     - Systems thinking principles
     - Strategic analysis techniques
     - Technology portfolio management
   - Enterprise Architecture Planning (EAP)

     - Integration and alignment strategies
   - Value Chain Analysis (VCA)

3. **Enterprise Architecture Frameworks**

   - Zachman Framework
     - Multi-dimensional classification schema
     - Stakeholder perspectives and aspects

4. **Planning Process and Phases**

   - Phase 3: Future State Design
     - To-Be architecture and vision

     - Solution design principles
   - Phase 4: Migration Planning

   - Phase 5: Implementation and Governance

     - Continuous improvement

5. **Requirements Analysis and Modeling**

   - Functional and non-functional requirements

6. **Technology Selection and Evaluation**
   - Technology assessment criteria
   - Vendor evaluation and selection
   - Proof of concept and pilot programs
   - Build vs. buy vs. outsource decisions

7. **Implementation Strategy**
   - Phased vs. big-bang approaches
   - Agile and iterative implementation
   - Change management and user adoption

8. **Governance, Risk, and Compliance**
   - IT governance frameworks (COBIT, ITIL)
   - Risk identification and mitigation
   - Security and privacy considerations
   - Compliance requirements (GDPR, SOX, etc.)
   - Audit and continuous monitoring

9. **Performance Measurement and Optimization**
   - KPI definition and tracking
   - Benefits realization management
   - ROI calculation and TCO analysis
   - Continuous improvement mechanisms
   - Post-implementation review

10. **Case Studies and Best Practices**
    - Enterprise ERP implementation
    - Digital transformation initiatives
    - Cloud migration projects
    - Lessons learned and success factors

## E - Experiment (实验)
**English:**
Provide multiple perspectives and approaches:
1. Compare different planning methodologies (BSP vs. CSF vs. SST) with use case scenarios
2. Present alternative architecture frameworks and their applicability
3. Discuss trade-offs in implementation strategies (agile vs. waterfall, cloud vs. on-premise)
4. Offer various governance models for different organizational structures
5. Include case studies from multiple industries (finance, healthcare, manufacturing, retail)
6. Present both top-down (strategic) and bottom-up (tactical) planning approaches
7. Explore modern trends (cloud-native, microservices, AI/ML integration)
8. Provide decision trees and selection criteria for methodology and technology choices

**中文:**
提供多种视角和方法：
1. 比较不同规划方法论（BSP vs. CSF vs. SST）及其用例场景
2. 展示替代架构框架及其适用性
3. 讨论实施策略中的权衡（敏捷vs.瀑布、云vs.本地）
4. 为不同组织结构提供各种治理模型
5. 包含来自多个行业的案例研究（金融、医疗、制造、零售）
6. 提供自上而下（战略）和自下而上（战术）规划方法
7. 探索现代趋势（云原生、微服务、AI/ML集成）
8. 为方法论和技术选择提供决策树和选择标准
