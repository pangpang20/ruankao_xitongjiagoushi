## R (Insight) | 背景洞察
Computer performance metrics are critical for evaluating system efficiency and user experience. The three fundamental metrics are:

1. **Response Time**: The time elapsed from when a request is submitted until the first response is received
2. **Throughput**: The amount of work completed per unit time
3. **Turnaround Time (TAT)**: The total time from job submission to job completion

These metrics are interdependent and often present trade-offs. Understanding how to calculate and optimize them is essential for software engineers preparing for professional certification exams and for practical system design.

计算机性能指标对于评估系统效率和用户体验至关重要。三个基本指标是：

1. **响应时间**：从提交请求到收到第一个响应所经过的时间
2. **吞吐量**：单位时间内完成的工作量
3. **周转时间（TAT）**：从作业提交到作业完成的总时间

这些指标相互依存，通常存在权衡关系。理解如何计算和优化这些指标对于准备专业认证考试的软件工程师以及实际系统设计都至关重要。
