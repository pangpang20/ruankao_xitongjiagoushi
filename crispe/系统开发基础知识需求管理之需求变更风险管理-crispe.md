## 1. Requirements Change Management Overview | 需求变更管理概述

### 1.1 Concept of Requirements Change | 需求变更的概念

**Requirements Change** refers to any modification, addition, or deletion to the agreed-upon requirements baseline during the software development lifecycle.

**需求变更**是指在软件开发生命周期中，对已达成一致的需求基线进行的任何修改、增加或删除。

**Key Characteristics | 关键特征**：
- Inevitability | 不可避免性：需求变更在项目中不可避免
- Impact | 影响性：变更会影响项目的范围、时间、成本和质量
- Traceability | 可追溯性：需求变更必须可追溯和可控
- Risk | 风险性：变更可能引入新的风险

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: Understanding the difference between requirement change and scope creep, and the role of CCB in change control.

---

### 2.3 Change Control Board (CCB) | 变更控制委员会

| Role             | English  | 中文     | Responsibility                  | 职责               |
| ---------------- | -------- | -------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| **Chairperson**  | 主席     | 主席     | Lead change review meetings     | 主持变更评审会议   |
| **Requirements** | 需求代表 | 需求代表 | Assess requirements impact      | 评估需求影响       |
| **Development**  | 开发代表 | 开发代表 | Assess technical feasibility    | 评估技术可行性     |
| **Testing**      | 测试代表 | 测试代表 | Assess testing impact           | 评估测试影响       |
| **Project Mgr**  | 项目经理 | 项目经理 | Assess schedule and cost impact | 评估进度和成本影响 |

**Decision Criteria | 决策标准**:
- Business Value vs. Cost | 业务价值 vs. 成本
- Risk Level | 风险等级
- Impact on Schedule | 对进度的影响
- Resource Availability | 资源可用性

---

### 3.2 Probability-Impact Matrix | 概率-影响矩阵

| Probability<br/>概率 | Impact: Low<br/>影响:低 | Impact: Medium<br/>影响:中 | Impact: High<br/>影响:高 |
| -------------------- | ----------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **High<br/>高**      | Medium Risk<br/>中风险  | High Risk<br/>高风险       | Critical Risk<br/>严重   |
| **Medium<br/>中**    | Low Risk<br/>低风险     | Medium Risk<br/>中风险     | High Risk<br/>高风险     |
| **Low<br/>低**       | Very Low<br/>很低       | Low Risk<br/>低风险        | Medium Risk<br/>中风险   |

**Risk Score Calculation | 风险分值计算**:
- Risk Score = Probability × Impact | 风险分值 = 概率 × 影响
- Example: P(0.7) × I(0.8) = 0.56 (High Risk) | 示例: P(0.7) × I(0.8) = 0.56 (高风险)
