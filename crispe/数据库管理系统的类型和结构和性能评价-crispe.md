## R - Insight (洞察)
**English:**
Understanding database management systems requires a comprehensive view that encompasses:
- **Type Classification**: Different DBMS types serve different use cases - relational databases excel at structured data with complex queries, while NoSQL databases provide flexibility and horizontal scalability for specific data models
- **Architectural Components**: The structure of a DBMS (storage engine, query processor, transaction manager, buffer manager) directly impacts its performance characteristics
- **Performance Trade-offs**: Every architectural decision involves trade-offs between consistency, availability, partition tolerance (CAP theorem), and performance metrics
- **Evaluation Context**: Performance evaluation must consider workload patterns, data characteristics, hardware resources, and specific business requirements

**中文:**
理解数据库管理系统需要全面的视角，包括:
- **类型分类**: 不同的DBMS类型服务于不同的用例 - 关系型数据库擅长处理结构化数据和复杂查询，而NoSQL数据库为特定数据模型提供灵活性和水平扩展能力
- **架构组件**: DBMS的结构(存储引擎、查询处理器、事务管理器、缓冲区管理器)直接影响其性能特性
- **性能权衡**: 每个架构决策都涉及一致性、可用性、分区容错性(CAP定理)和性能指标之间的权衡
- **评估上下文**: 性能评估必须考虑工作负载模式、数据特征、硬件资源和具体业务需求
