## 1. International IT Standards | 国际信息化标准体系

### 1.1 ISO/IEC Standards Framework | ISO/IEC标准体系

**ISO/IEC (International Organization for Standardization/International Electrotechnical Commission)** is the world's largest developer of voluntary international standards, providing a framework for IT systems, security, and management.

**ISO/IEC（国际标准化组织/国际电工委员会）**是世界上最大的自愿性国际标准开发者，为IT系统、安全和管理提供框架。

**Key Standard Categories | 主要标准类别**:

| Standard Category        | English Name                                                      | Chinese Name             | Purpose                                 | 用途                       |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | --------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| **ISO/IEC 27000 Series** | Information Security Management                                   | 信息安全管理             | Establish and maintain ISMS             | 建立和维护信息安全管理体系 |
| **ISO/IEC 20000 Series** | IT Service Management                                             | IT服务管理               | Implement ITSM based on ISO 9000        | 基于ISO 9000实施IT服务管理 |
| **ISO/IEC 12207**        | Software Life Cycle Processes                                     | 软件生命周期过程         | Define software engineering processes   | 定义软件工程过程           |
| **ISO/IEC 15408**        | Common Criteria                                                   | 通用准则                 | Evaluate IT security                    | 评估IT安全性               |
| **ISO/IEC 25000 Series** | Systems and Software Quality Requirements and Evaluation (SQuaRE) | 系统和软件质量要求和评价 | Define software quality characteristics | 定义软件质量特性           |

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: Master the main ISO/IEC standards and their purposes; ISO 27001 and ISO 20000 are high-frequency exam points.

### 1.2 ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management | ISO/IEC 27001信息安全管理

**ISO/IEC 27001:2013** is an international standard for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

**ISO/IEC 27001:2013** 是建立、实施、维护和持续改进信息安全管理体系（ISMS）的国际标准。

**Core Requirements | 核心要求**:
- **Leadership and commitment | 领导力与承诺**: Top management must demonstrate commitment to ISMS | 高层管理必须展示对ISMS的承诺
- **Risk assessment and treatment | 风险评估与处理**: Systematic risk management process | 系统性风险管理过程
- **Control objectives and controls | 控制目标与控制措施**: 114 controls in 14 categories (Annex A) | 14个类别中的114项控制措施（附录A）
- **Performance evaluation | 绩效评价**: Monitor, measure, analyze and evaluate | 监控、测量、分析和评价
- **Improvement | 持续改进**: Continual improvement of ISMS | ISMS持续改进

**PDCA Cycle in ISO 27001 | ISO 27001的PDCA循环**:

```
┌─────────────────┐
│     Plan        │
│  (风险评估)      │
│  (Risk Assessment) │
└─────────┬───────┘
          │
┌─────────▼───────┐
│    Do           │
│  (实施控制)      │
│  (Implement Controls) │
└─────────┬───────┘
          │
┌─────────▼───────┐
│   Check         │
│  (监控评估)      │
│  (Monitor & Evaluate) │
└─────────┬───────┘
          │
┌─────────▼───────┐
│   Act           │
│  (改进措施)      │
│  (Take Action)  │
└─────────────────┘
```

### 1.3 ISO/IEC 20000 IT Service Management | ISO/IEC 20000 IT服务管理

**ISO/IEC 20000** is the international standard for IT service management (ITSM), based on the IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL) framework.

**ISO/IEC 20000** 是基于IT基础架构库（ITIL）框架的IT服务管理（ITSM）国际标准。

**Key Processes | 关键流程**:

| Process Area                 | English Name | Chinese Name | Purpose                                                    | 用途                           |
| ---------------------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Service Level Management** | 服务级别管理 | 服务级别管理 | Define, agree, monitor and report service levels           | 定义、协商、监控和报告服务级别 |
| **Incident Management**      | 事件管理     | 事件管理     | Restore normal service operation as quickly as possible    | 尽快恢复正常的客户服务运营     |
| **Problem Management**       | 问题管理     | 问题管理     | Prevent incidents from happening and eliminate root causes | 防止事件发生并消除根本原因     |
| **Change Management**        | 变更管理     | 变更管理     | Ensure changes are implemented with minimum disruption     | 确保变更实施时干扰最小         |
| **Configuration Management** | 配置管理     | 配置管理     | Maintain accurate configuration information                | 维护准确的配置信息             |

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: Understand the differences between incident management and problem management; know the key processes of IT service management.

## 2. China IT Laws and Regulations | 中国信息化法律法规

### 2.1 Fundamental Laws | 基础性法律

#### 2.1.1 Cybersecurity Law of China | 中华人民共和国网络安全法

**Full Name | 全称**: Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China | 中华人民共和国网络安全法

**Enactment Date | 颁布日期**: November 7, 2016 | 2016年11月7日

**Effective Date | 施行日期**: June 1, 2017 | 2017年6月1日

**Key Provisions | 核心条款**:

| Article           | Content                           | 内容             | Key Points                                                                    | 关键点                                 |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Article 21**    | Network Security Level Protection | 网络安全等级保护 | Critical information infrastructure operators must implement level protection | 关键信息基础设施运营者必须实施等级保护 |
| **Article 37**    | Data Localization                 | 数据本地化       | Personal information and important data must be stored in China               | 个人信息和重要数据必须境内存储         |
| **Article 38**    | Security Assessment               | 安全评估         | Cross-border data transfer requires security assessment                       | 跨境数据传输需安全评估                 |
| **Article 41-43** | Personal Information Protection   | 个人信息保护     | Collect with consent, use for specified purposes                              | 收集需同意，使用需特定目的             |

**Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) | 关键信息基础设施**:
- Important networks and information systems related to national security | 涉及国家安全的重要网络和信息系统
- Important networks and information systems that may seriously harm national security, economic well-being, or public interests if damaged | 损坏后可能严重危害国家安全、国计民生、公共利益的重要网络和信息系统

**Obligations of CII Operators | CII运营者义务**:
- Implement technical measures to monitor and record network operation status and cybersecurity incidents | 采取技术措施监测、记录网络运行状态和网络安全事件
- Implement data classification, backup, and encryption measures | 实施数据分类、备份和加密措施
- Regularly provide security and risk assessments to regulatory authorities | 定期向有关主管部门提供安全和风险评估

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: Master the key provisions of the Cybersecurity Law, especially data localization and level protection requirements; understand the definition and obligations of CII operators.

### 3.2 Classification and Filing Process | 定级与备案流程

**Classification Process | 定级流程**:

**Step 1: Determine Classification Object | 确定定级对象**
- Identify the system to be classified | 识别需要定级的系统
- Determine the scope of the system | 确定系统范围
- Assess the business importance | 评估业务重要性

**Step 2: Analyze Business Information Security | 分析业务信息安全**
- Identify business information assets | 识别业务信息资产
- Assess the impact of information destruction | 评估信息破坏的影响
- Determine information security level | 确定信息安全等级

**Step 3: Analyze System Service Security | 分析系统服务安全**
- Identify system service assets | 识别系统服务资产
- Assess the impact of service disruption | 评估服务中断的影响
- Determine system service security level | 确定系统服务安全等级

**Step 4: Determine Final Level | 确定最终等级**
- Take the higher of the two levels (information security vs. service security) | 取两者中较高的等级（信息安全与服务安全）
- Final level = max(Information Security Level, Service Security Level) | 最终等级 = max(信息安全等级, 服务安全等级)

**Filing Process | 备案流程**:

| Step  | Action                              | 行为         | Responsible Party         | 责任方     |
| ----- | ----------------------------------- | ------------ | ------------------------- | ---------- |
| **1** | Prepare filing materials            | 准备备案材料 | System operator           | 系统运营者 |
| **2** | Submit to public security authority | 提交公安机关 | System operator           | 系统运营者 |
| **3** | Review by public security           | 公安机关审查 | Public security authority | 公安机关   |
| **4** | Issue filing certificate            | 颁发备案证明 | Public security authority | 公安机关   |

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: Understand the classification process and how to determine the final level; know the filing process steps.

### 3.3 Security Construction and Rectification | 安全建设与整改

**Security Construction Principles | 安全建设原则**:

3. **Dynamic Adjustment | 动态调整**: Adjust security measures based on changing threats | 根据变化的威胁调整安全措施

4. **Compliance-Driven | 合规驱动**: Meet level protection requirements | 满足等级保护要求

**Security Control Categories | 安全控制类别**:

### 6.2 Typical Case Analysis | 典型案例分析

**Case 1: Financial Institution Data Protection | 金融机构数据保护**

**Background | 背景**:
A large Chinese bank plans to transfer customer transaction data to its overseas parent company for risk analysis. The data includes personal information of 1 million customers and transaction records.

某大型中资银行计划将客户交易数据传输至海外母公司进行风险分析。数据包括100万客户的个人信息和交易记录。

**Question | 问题**:

1. What IP protection strategies should be used? | 应采用哪些知识产权保护策略？
2. What are the advantages and limitations of each strategy? | 各策略的优势和局限性是什么？
3. How to implement these strategies? | 如何实施这些策略？

**Answer | 答案**:
1. **IP Protection Strategies | 知识产权保护策略**:
   - **Copyright**: Protect source code and documentation | 著作权：保护源代码和文档
   - **Patent**: Protect innovative algorithms and technical solutions | 专利：保护创新算法和技术方案
   - **Trademark**: Protect brand name and logo | 商标：保护品牌名称和标志
   - **Trade Secret**: Protect business logic and implementation details | 商业秘密：保护业务逻辑和实现细节

2. **Advantages and Limitations | 优势和局限性**:

| Strategy         | Advantages                        | 优势             | Limitations                            | 局限性           |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------- | -------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| **Copyright**    | Automatic protection, low cost    | 自动保护，成本低 | No protection for ideas and algorithms | 不保护思想和算法 |
| **Patent**       | Strong protection for innovations | 对创新强保护     | High cost, lengthy process             | 成本高，流程长   |
| **Trademark**    | Brand protection                  | 品牌保护         | Limited to brand elements              | 限于品牌元素     |
| **Trade Secret** | No registration required          | 无需注册         | Difficult to prove infringement        | 难以证明侵权     |

3. **Implementation Approach | 实施方式**:
   - **Copyright**: Register software copyright voluntarily | 自愿登记软件著作权
   - **Patent**: File patent applications for core algorithms | 对核心算法申请专利
   - **Trademark**: Register company and product trademarks | 注册公司和产品商标
   - **Trade Secret**: Implement confidentiality agreements and access controls | 实施保密协议和访问控制
