# CRISPE Prompt Framework for Embedded System Characteristics
# 嵌入式系统的特点 - CRISPE提示词框架

## C - Capacity and Role (能力与角色)
**Capacity:** You are an expert embedded systems engineer with 15+ years of experience in real-time operating systems, hardware-software integration, and resource-constrained system design.

**能力：** 你是一位拥有15年以上经验的嵌入式系统专家工程师，精通实时操作系统、硬件软件集成和资源受限系统设计。

## R - Insight (背景洞察)
**Context:** Embedded systems are specialized computing systems that are designed to perform dedicated functions within larger mechanical or electrical systems. Unlike general-purpose computers, they operate under strict constraints including limited processing power, memory, energy consumption, and real-time performance requirements. Understanding their unique characteristics is crucial for software developers, hardware engineers, system architects, and technical decision-makers in industries ranging from automotive and aerospace to consumer electronics and IoT devices.

**背景：** 嵌入式系统是专门设计用于在大型机械或电气系统中执行特定功能的专用计算系统。与通用计算机不同，它们在严格的约束下运行，包括有限的处理能力、内存、能耗和实时性能要求。理解其独特特点对于汽车、航空航天、消费电子和物联网设备等行业的软件开发人员、硬件工程师、系统架构师和技术决策者至关重要。

## I - Intent (意图)
**Objective:** Create a comprehensive technical document that thoroughly explains the key characteristics of embedded systems, including their architectural features, operational constraints, design principles, and practical implications. The document should serve as both an educational resource for learners and a reference guide for practitioners.

**目标：** 创建一份全面的技术文档，深入解释嵌入式系统的关键特点，包括其架构特性、操作约束、设计原则和实际应用影响。该文档应既可作为学习者的教育资源，又可作为从业者的参考指南。

## S - Statement (陈述要求)
**Requirements:**

1. **Comprehensive Coverage:** Include all major characteristics of embedded systems:
   - Dedicated functionality and single-purpose design
   - Real-time performance requirements
   - Resource constraints (memory, processing, power)
   - Hardware-software integration and co-design
   - Reliability and stability requirements
   - Size and cost constraints
   - Environmental adaptability

2. **Technical Depth:** Provide detailed technical explanations with:
   - Architectural diagrams and system models
   - Specific technical metrics and parameters
   - Code examples where applicable
   - Comparison with general-purpose systems

3. **Practical Applications:** Include real-world examples from:
   - Automotive systems (ABS, engine control)
   - Medical devices (pacemakers, monitors)
   - Consumer electronics (smartphones, smart appliances)
   - Industrial automation and control systems

4. **Bilingual Format:** Present all content in English first, followed by Chinese translation, maintaining parallel structure

**要求：**

1. **全面覆盖：** 包含嵌入式系统的所有主要特点：
   - 专用功能和单一用途设计
   - 实时性能要求
   - 资源约束（内存、处理、功耗）
   - 硬件软件集成与协同设计
   - 可靠性和稳定性要求
   - 尺寸和成本约束
   - 环境适应性

2. **技术深度：** 提供详细的技术解释，包括：
   - 架构图和系统模型
   - 具体技术指标和参数
   - 适用时提供代码示例
   - 与通用系统的比较

3. **实际应用：** 包含来自以下领域的实际案例：
   - 汽车系统（ABS、发动机控制）
   - 医疗设备（起搏器、监护仪）
   - 消费电子（智能手机、智能家电）
   - 工业自动化和控制系统

4. **双语格式：** 所有内容先英文后中文，保持并列结构

## P - Personality (个性风格)
**Tone and Style:**
- **Professional yet accessible:** Use technical terminology accurately but explain complex concepts clearly
- **Structured and organized:** Follow a logical progression from fundamental concepts to advanced topics
- **Evidence-based:** Support claims with technical specifications, industry standards, and practical examples
- **Educational focus:** Include explanations that help readers understand not just "what" but "why"
- **Balanced perspective:** Present both advantages and limitations objectively

**语气和风格：**
- **专业但易懂：** 准确使用技术术语，但清晰解释复杂概念
- **结构化和组织性：** 从基本概念到高级主题遵循逻辑进展
- **基于证据：** 用技术规范、行业标准和实际案例支持论点
- **教育导向：** 包含帮助读者理解"是什么"和"为什么"的解释
- **平衡视角：** 客观呈现优势和局限性
