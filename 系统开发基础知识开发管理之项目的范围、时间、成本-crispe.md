## 1. Project Scope Management | 项目范围管理

### 1.1 Scope Management Concepts | 范围管理概念

**Project Scope Management** includes the processes required to ensure that the project includes all the work required, and only the work required, to complete the project successfully.

**项目范围管理**包括确保项目包含且仅包含成功完成项目所需工作的过程。

**Key Processes | 关键过程**：
- Plan Scope Management | 规划范围管理
- Collect Requirements | 收集需求
- Define Scope | 定义范围
- Create WBS | 创建WBS
- Validate Scope | 确认范围
- Control Scope | 控制范围

**[Exam Focus | 考试重点]**: The relationship between project scope and product scope, and the importance of WBS in scope management.

### 2.3 Critical Path Method | 关键路径法

| Technique             | English      | 中文         | Purpose                                | 目的                   |
| --------------------- | ------------ | ------------ | -------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Forward Pass**      | 前向计算     | 前向计算     | Calculate early start and early finish | 计算最早开始和最早完成 |
| **Backward Pass**     | 后向计算     | 后向计算     | Calculate late start and late finish   | 计算最晚开始和最晚完成 |
| **Float Calculation** | 浮动时间计算 | 浮动时间计算 | Determine activity flexibility         | 确定活动灵活性         |

**Example Calculation | 示例计算**:
- ES (Early Start) = Maximum of all preceding EF | ES = 所有前置EF的最大值
- EF (Early Finish) = ES + Duration | EF = ES + 持续时间
- LS (Late Start) = LF - Duration | LS = LF - 持续时间
- LF (Late Finish) = Minimum of all succeeding LS | LF = 所有后续LS的最小值
